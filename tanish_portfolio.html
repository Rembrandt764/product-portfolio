🦻 Digital Health · AI Companion · Live Demo

Here — AI Hearing Aid Onboarding

The first 30 days with a hearing aid are brutal — your brain is overwhelmed by everyday sounds, and most patients quit. Here is an AI-powered onboarding companion that bridges the gap between clinic and home. Personal project born from my own experience as someone 45% hearing-impaired.

Features daily wear goals, emoji comfort check-ins, an AI feedback loop that autonomously fine-tunes the sound profile (rate-limited to 3x/day), and a clinician-facing triage dashboard with remote EQ controls. Designed to cut device abandonment from 30% to under 10%.
